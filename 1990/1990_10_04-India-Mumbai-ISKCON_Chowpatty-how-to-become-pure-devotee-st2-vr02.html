[00:00:00 to 00:00:08 inaudible]

So, I was asked to speak a few words tonight and what was the topic you decided that I should speak on? [00:00:17-00:00:21 inaudible] You want me to speak on how to become a pure devotee! Ah! this the most important question in our life.

It is not that we must become a pure devotee because we already are pure devotees. Just as I will give an example; If a young man has a father who is a millionaire, and the child falls asleep and he is dreaming that he is a beggar on the street and in his dream, he is thinking how can I become wealthy. The fact is, he already is wealthy, the fortune of his father already belongs to him, but he has forgotten. Due to ignorance in his dream, he has forgotten the wealth of his father, he has forgotten his connection with his father and therefore he is actually suffering due to poverty. So, it is not that he has to become rich, he already is rich, but he has to wake up and understand the truth of who he is and his relationship with his father. For another example, similar, if a young man has a father who has put one million dollars in bank account for him but he forgets who is father is and he forgets his account number and therefore he is begging in the street, he is starving, he is suffering and he is thinking how to become rich, how come to… how to eat nice food? He already is rich but the because of forgetfulness he is suffering, so that is our condition in this material world. It is said in Gita,

mamaivāṁśo jīva-loke

jīva-bhūtaḥ sanātanaḥ

manaḥ-ṣaṣṭhānīndriyāṇi

prakṛti-sthāni karṣati

[Bg. 15.7]

[The living entities in this conditioned world are My eternal fragmental parts. Due to conditioned life, they are struggling very hard with the six senses, which include the mind.]

That we are all part of God. We are all of same quality as God himself and what are God’s qualities he is ‘sat-cit-ananda’, He is eternal of full of knowledge and full of bliss and similarly the ‘atma’ the soul is eternal full of knowledge and full of bliss and because we are part and parcel of Supreme atma the Paramatma or Krishna, therefore pure devotion to God is the natural tendency within all of our hearts.

Nature of every soul is to love God.

It is explained by Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu,

nitya-siddha kṛṣṇa-prema ‘sādhya’ kabhu naya

śravaṇādi-śuddha-citte karaye udaya

[Cc. Madhya 22.107]

[Pure love for Kṛṣṇa is eternally established in the hearts of the living entities. It is not something to be gained from another source. When the heart is purified by hearing and chanting, this love naturally awakens.]

That love of God, pure devotion is already in the heart of every living being not only each and every one of us in this world, in this room, who are anxious to understand higher principles of philosophy. But also, the common man, who is working so hard just earn some money in this world, whether they be Americans, Russians, Pakistanis, Africans, Iraqis, whether they be elephant, cows, dogs or insects. Wherever there is the life force, wherever there is consciousness, that is the symptoms of the presence of the soul and then the nature of every soul which is part of the Supreme soul is to love God. We are all pure, we all are pure devotees of God but different species of life and different people in different type of consciousness are due to different layers of covering of forgetfulness which obscure the pure devotional that is within our heart. Therefore, Lord Caitanya had said that pure devotion is within the heart of everyone, every living entity! Now how to awaken that pure devotion? Lord Caitanya has given a simple formula by simply chanting the name of God and associating with pure devotees of God, that natural love of God is awaken within our heart. Like the sun the sun is always in the sky sometimes we see it and sometimes we do not but the sun is always there, during the monsoon rains sometimes the sun is covered by clouds and when the cloud is removed the sun reveals everything and right now, we do not see the sun it is night but does that mean the sun is cease to exist the sun is existing in another place and because it is covered, we are not able to perceive it.

So, Krishna consciousness is the original natural consciousness within everyone’s hearts and in enlightenment meant simply to remove the covering of darkness and allow light of soul to shine through within us. So how to allow the natural light of the soul, the natural devotion the pure devotion within our, to shine from within us? This is the most important question of the human form of life, in fact in the Brahma Sutra it is said, ‘athatto bhrama jigyasa’ that human life begins when we asks this question, what is our relationship with God? how to develop,? How to awaken our pure devotion to God in this life? Until we sincerely ask this question, the quality of our life is like that of the animals. simply existing, eating, sleeping, mating and defending and making so many grand arrangements around these very very base principles of survival. Human life is very rare because human life affords the opportunity to become enlightened to wake up.

Process of awakening comes through the process of hearing and chanting

Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu every morning he would gather his devotees together and they would go into the streets and they would have Kirtan and they would sing very beautiful song,

jīv jāgo, jīv jāgo, gauracānda bole

kota nidrā jāo māyā-piśācīra kole

[Aruṇodaya-kīrtana II]

[Lord Gaurāṅga is calling, "Wake up, sleeping souls! Wake up, sleeping souls! You have slept so long on the lap of the witch Māyā!]

‘Wake up sleeping souls wake up! you are all sleeping in the lap of witch of maya and therefore you are suffering the turmoil’s of material creations. Wake up! come out of the nightmare situation of yours, wake up understand that you are eternal full of knowledge full of bliss; understand that you are part of God, that your father is Supreme almighty opulent person, who is the controller of all the controllers wake up re-enter again into your eternal family in the presence of God.’

So, just like in the morning; If you are having a very bad dream, your beloved family members when they see you tossing and turning and rolling around, they feel great sympathy for you they know that you are safe, they know you are peacefully situated in nice protected house but you are suffering like anything because you do not know it, because you are identifying with a body in a dream which is not you which has nothing to do with you, you are lying in a nice bed and everything around you is very nice, your body is young, healthy what is the problem? But you are dreaming that you are falling from a mountain, and you are about to crash into a ground and die and this great fear and you are screaming and crying no no no no and your family members are all around you and they feel great… they feel pain in their hearts that you are suffering due to this illusion. So, they wake you up and how did they wake you up? by sound vibration! ‘Ah’? Just like we have alarm clocks. That is the most popular way of waking up. Correct? Alarm clock? Now what is an alarm clock? The fact is that the number and the hands are on clock; why do you need an alarm? Because you cannot see the number on the hands. If you want to wake up at 5 o’clock in the morning, it’s not that when you dial its five you go ah! Now it’s time to wake up! Because in a sleeping condition you cannot see, you cannot taste, often times you cannot feel, but there is one… you cannot smell. There is one sense that remains awake even in our sleeping condition; that is the sense of hearing, ah! therefore when the alarm rings. ‘tring, tring, tring’ we go now ‘aa… aa… aaa…’ so now I am awake.

So similarly, when the family members seeing that we are suffering a nightmare and we are tossing and turning; through sound vibration they call our name ‘wake up, wakeup, wakeup’ and then you wake up and when you wake up you realize aahh! there’s never been a problem everything is alright. So therefore, in this world we find in all the great religions of the world and all the great scriptures of the world that the process of awakening comes through the process of hearing and chanting. Just like the Bhagavad-Gita. Arjuna felt into the slumber of ignorance in the first chapter of Gita I believe each week Sri Govinda is giving lecture on Bhagavad-gita.

In the footstep of Arjuna you have to humble yourself at the feet of God or the Bonafide representative of God

Arjuna who is a perfect devotee of God, he was Krishna’s best friend was he not? but somehow other due to the circumstances around him, he forgot his relationship with Krishna. He forgot his elevated position and in this forgetfulness, he was suffering horrible, tears were flowing from his eyes, his body was trembling, his… his limbs trembled, his complexion became pale white, his mouth became parch dry. He was such in miserable condition and in his miserable condition he approached Krishna, ‘Please instruct me I do not know what to do.’ At first, he was thinking, I know how to solve my problems in life, I am in miserable condition and he began to use very very good intelligence and he had a very excellent intelligence; How to solve his problem and he came to so many conclusions and he began to present all these conclusions to Krishna, “I will not fight this war! If I fight this war, then so many people will die. Why? Just because the Pandavas want to be the kings? So many people die people like my Guru Dhronacharya, my Grandfather Bhishma, respected Personalities! Even if I gain the kingdom, if it is… if my… if my treasures are tainted with the blood of those who I love how can I enjoy it therefore I will not fight. And not only that but if we fight this war so many people will die and when they die their wife’s they will have no one to protect them and women are not protected unscrupulous man take advantage of them and exploit them. And when women are exploited by unscrupulous man then there is unwanted population as result. And when there is unwanted population, such progeny does not perform sacrifice, does not engage in spiritual or religious activities and with their presence as the future generation the whole society is condemned, the so… society is demned… is condemned to sin and frustration and suffering! Therefore, I will not fight this war.”

But even with all of his planning, even with all of his solutions the anguish in his heart was only increasing and then he said, “Krishna, I do not know anything!”

kārpaṇya-doṣopahata-svabhāvaḥ

pṛcchāmi tvāṁ dharma-sammūḍha-cetāḥ

yac chreyaḥ syān niścitaṁ brūhi tan me

śiṣyas te ’haṁ śādhi māṁ tvāṁ prapannam

[Bg. 2.7]

[Now I am confused about my duty and have lost all composure because of miserly weakness. In this condition I am asking You to tell me for certain what is best for me. Now I am Your disciple, and a soul surrendered unto You. Please instruct me.]

That Krishna, “I am now confused about what is my duty what is to be done and what is not to be done, what is religion and what is irreligion. I have lost all composure due to weakness. Now Krishna please you instruct me I am your disciple, and I surrendered to you.”

Your question is, ‘How to become a pure devotee, how to wake up the true glory of the self.’ The Bhagavad-gita by example of Arjuna illustrates. The first step is you must keep aside all your preconceived ideas of what is truth, what is reality and what is the solutions to the problem of the world. And you have to, in the footstep of Arjuna you have to humble yourself at the feet of God or the Bonafide representative of God the spiritual master or the pure Sadhu. And when we approach in the same mood of Arjuna, ‘I know nothing, whether I have a PhD or ten PhDs, when it comes to the spiritual subject matter of life, I know nothing, when it comes to practically even if I have studied religion’. I have seen people with PhDs in religion but they know nothing about to practically awaken their soul. It is not by academic knowledge that you can understand the truths of the Vedas, the Vedas explains tad vijnanartham sa gurum evabhigacchet (Mundaka Upanisad 1.2.12) That, one can only understand the truth of Vedas by the process of approaching a proper professor of the Vedas. Ah! Sri Govinda is a surgeon and he is teaching surgery for many years he was teaching surgery at J.J hospital. Why did you need somebody to teach you like this? The fact is to get into a college is very difficult you have to work very hard and pass so many tests. The same text books you can just buy at a book store and go home and read them. But will you ever learn surgery by sitting home reading your text books? Those text books have to be explained practically according to time and circumstances to you by someone who is experienced and has realized the subject matter. Ah? So similarly, if we want to understand the truth, we have to hear it from the lips of the self-realized souls, that is the first and most important step in the process of self-realization and

tad viddhi praṇipātena

paripraśnena sevayā

upadekṣyanti te jṣānaṁ

jṣāninas tattva-darśinaḥ

[Bg. 4.34]

[Just try to learn the truth by approaching a spiritual master. Inquire from him submissively and render service unto him. The self-realized souls can impart knowledge unto you because they have seen the truth.]

Krishna says if you want to know the truth you have to approach Guru and you have to inquire submissively and render service. In this way the self-realized soul can impart knowledge on to you because he has seen the truth. So not only do we have to approach a self-realized soul, but we must approach him in a proper spirit. Just like if a ahh… if a gardener wants to grow from a seed a mango tree, the first thing he has to do is he has to prepare the soil ah! or let us say you want to grow ahh… whatever whether it is mango or whether it is rice or whether it is wheat first You have to plough the field, first you have to fertilize the field then you put the seed and then it will grow, if you put the seed in sand will it grow? If you put the seed amongst rocks, will it grow?

So simply coming and hearing the transcendental subject matter which comes through the process of hearing into our hearts is like the seed of pure devotional service, but our heart has to be a fertile field for that seed to grow. And the fertility of the heart is according to how we are hearing submissively, not challenging, not academically but with the true desire to know to understand and to practice.

Natural condition to reach to Krishna consciousness

Arjuna when he was settling on the battlefield of Kurukshetra between the two armies he wasn’t simply listening to some theoretical discourse by Sri Krishna, was he? His ears and his hearts and his mind was so open to receive what Krishna was saying. It was a matter of life and death to him to understand the subject of Gita was it not, to understand? It was an urgent emergency I must understand Krishna instruct me please and his ears were thirsty for knowledge and his heart was starving for knowledge.

Sometimes we would approach Srila Prabhupada because after all on his order we were out eight in the morning sometimes till nine or ten at night just trying to convince people to wake up. Ah? And we come back sometime and we will say, “Srila Prabhupada, we approach people and tell them to wakeup that this material world is a place of suffering and they say, ‘But I am not suffering, I am very happy, I am very peacefully, what do I… I do not need this religion, I do not need this God’, what to tell these people?” Srila Prabhupada said, “Don’t bother with these people just give them some Prasada. As long as a person thinks that he is happy what can you tell him.” Did you ever try to preach to a man who is drunk and intoxicated? We have seen a man is so drastically intoxicated and he is laying in a hole in the ground, his head is cut open, blood is pouring from everywhere, his clothes are torn up, his limbs are broken and he so intoxicated when we say, ‘let me help you out of this whole’, He says, “no no no I am enjoying, I am enjoying, leave me alone”. And when you try to help him, he goes to punch you, kicks you, ‘leave me alone I am enjoying.’ How can… What can you do to help such a man? he is in such an intoxicated state, you have to wait till he become little more sober and when he realizes he is suffering then he will reach out and say, ‘Please help, help take me out of this mess.’

Similarly, as long as Arjuna was intoxicated with the pride that he knew how to solve his problems; Krishna did not say a single word to him. Ah! In Gita try to understand as long as Arjuna was talking, thinking he knew… he knew what is what Krishna remain utterly silent, he did not waste even a single breath to speak to him. It was not until Arjuna sobered up and realize that he was suffering and said, ‘Krishna save me please’, then his heart was a fertile field because he became submissive. He understood the urgency for Krishna consciousness and then Krishna place that seed within his heart, the seed of knowledge. And that seed entered through his ears through the process of hearing so this is the beginning of spiritual life.

When we have a genuine desire with essence of urgency to understand and to practice the means which will take me to our natural condition of Krishna consciousness. So therefore, Krishna says, ‘tad viddhi praṇipātena paripraśnena sevayā’ [Bg. 4.34] If you want to learn the truth first you must inquire submissively as did Arjuna. Not challenging, at the beginning you can challenge Guru just to find out whether he is Guru, just to understand with your mind to be convinced that this is the good thing this is the truth. So that challenging may be good at the beginning, but when we have some conviction that yes this is Guru and this is truth then all challenging must be thrown aside, its purpose has been served. Then with folded palms we have to became submissive as Arjuna, ‘Now I am your disciple please instruct me!’

Taking care of the seed of Krishna Consciousness

And then next part is ‘paripraśnena sevayā’[Bg. 4.34] Seva means service. This means what we hear we must practically apply to our life. Seva is like water, through the process of hearing with the seed is planted in the fertile soil of our heart and through the process of rendering service that seed is daily watered and then it grows. But when the seed is very young and very small, try to understand that there are so many ways that it can be destroyed and disturbed Ah? When a seed is just growing if somebody steps on it its finished, when its very young,chilling if some animals comes and eats it, it is finished. So, while keeping the soil fertile through submissive hearing and association with saintly persons and watering it by rendering service we must also protect it and that protection takes place by following of regulative principles of freedom.

How to become a Pure devotee, principle of association

In the Srimad-Bhagavatam it describes, the essential four regulative principles are no illicit sex, no intoxication, no gambling, no meat eating. Of course, there are many other principles but these are the four basic sinful activities which very, very greatly disturbed the growth of the flower of devotion within our heart. So therefore, if we carefully follow the principles of devotional service and carefully water that seed then that natural inclination toward Krishna, toward devotional will grow within our heart. What you associate with you become like! this is a principle. This is a law of material nature it is not just our opinion. If you put a hot… a rod… iron rod in fire and leave it there what happens to that rod ah? It becomes hot like fire and it glows red like fire and if you touch it, you get burnt as much as you put the finger directly in the fire. Now if you take that same iron rod and put it in ice then what happens? It becomes freeze and cold like ice and if you touch it, you feel the same sensation as if you touch the ice directly, ah! So, the law of nature is whatever an object associates with it takes on those qualities.

So, the first most important thing we must understand is who we associate with we will become like that. If you want to become a pure devotee you must… you must associate with pure devotees and you must avoid the association of those persons who are living a life contrary to the principles of pure devotional service. Its most essential. Just like if you put the rod in fire for five minutes and then you put it on ice for ten minutes and then you put in fire for five minutes and then ice for ten minutes what happens to the rod? Nothing just it goes back and forth, back and forth and back and forth there is no progress either direction. So similarly, Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu has said, that, ‘If you want the seed of pure devotion to grow in your heart. ‘asat-saṅga-tyāga ei vaiṣṇava-ācāra’ [Cc. Madhya 22.87] you must associate with saintly person who are striving in that direction and you must avoid the association of those who are too much attached to material sense enjoyment. So how to become a Pure devotee? By associating with those who are Pure devotees and by associating with those who are striving for pure devotional service.

Now you may ask how it is possible after all I have family; I have my business or I have my profession I cannot just be living like these Brahmacharis amongst devotees day and night. So how can I practically associate with devotees and avoid the association of non-devotees? We must understand what is the principle of association. In battle of Kurukshetra, Arjuna factually physically in the association with his enemies, ah! But his mind was always fixed on carrying out the order of Krishna. He was always in the mission of Krishna even when he was in association with Duryodhana and all others, he was never pursuing their mission, ah! So therefore, in the execution of our duty we may have to associate with so many different types of people. How to remain a lotus flower in that condition? Do you know the story of lotus flower? Sometimes a lotus flower grows in a most muddy muddy ponds of water but the lotus flower is never touched by even one grave of dirt. Ah! Why is that? It is said, ‘Vande guroḥ śrī-caraṇāravindam’ [Gurv-aṣṭaka] The great souls, the great devotees of Lord they are like lotus flower, their feet are like lotus feet, because even though they are living in these worlds, where there are so much commotion, so much quarrel, so much hypocrisy, so much envy, and lust and greed and pride and anger, so much violence, so many materialistic tendencies. Such a person like a lotus flower even in pond is not at all disturbed is not even tinged by these, how is it possible? You see the secret of the lotus flower is that Its steam goes down, down, down, down, down through this pond of water into the ground under the water and its roots are in the soil and because of its roots are in the soil it is being nourished by the soil and therefore it is always indifferent to the condition of what the water is like around it.

So similarly, if we can always keep our consciousness which is like the steam connected through its roots to our source, the soil of Krishna, then even if we are in the most averse and horrible conditions of life like a lotus, we will be untainted. But if the steam of lotus is cut, if you separate the lotus from its connection to the ground, what happens? The lotus flower shakes, shrivels up and just becomes the part of the muddy water. So, we must keep always our connection to Krishna. Therefore, every morning we should rise early, and we should chant the holy names of the Lord. What we associate with we become like! The name of God is non different from God himself. It is the easiest and most practical means of keeping our consciousness with Krishna by chanting his name. Anyone can chant the name of God. He has made himself Supremely accessible through his name. Just like if you are in a middle of a crisis in a business, can you just take out Bhagavad-gita and start reading? Ah! But you can say ‘Hare Krishna’. If you are in a car and the car is coming going down to mountain and you about to die, do you start reading the four Vedas? Do you have time? is God accessible through that process or can you sit in a lotus posture and fix your and start raising your kundalini through the chakras? Ah? Is that process accessible? It is only one process access… accessible at that time ‘Hare Krishna’! And God is right there! He is with you in His divine presence and form.

Recently I was in naturopathic hospital and this doctor very very pious man, He was explaining the importance of fasting and he was of explaining importance of purifying the heart through… through fasting and through drinking grape juice and all these different types of cures. So we went to this one temple which was high, high on a hill and we were coming down very, very thin roads and it was night and the car that we are driving in, just started falling off and somehow other the driver looked it back and we looking down thinking my God and then I ask the doctor who is very, very umm… convinced of his tradition that through um… fasting, naturopathy, eating diet you can solve all the problems of life and I asked him, ‘Now do you admit that is very likely that it could have happened that we fell down that hill?’ And He said, ‘Yes we almost did. Its… It’s a miracle that we did not.’ I said, ‘All right now we were falling down that mountain and you knew you only have a few seconds to live in this life would you start drinking grape juice or would you cry out Krishna, Krishna save me’. He said, ‘I would call out Krishna, Krishna save me’. I said, ‘So you see of all processes there is nothing better than the chanting of Holy name’ [chuckles] And then every situation of life the name is there, accessible,

[Garuḍa Purāṇa]

[Either pure or impure, or having passed through all conditions of material life, if one can remember the lotus-eyed Kṛṣṇa, he becomes externally and internally clean.]

One who remembers the all pure becomes purified this is the law of association, just as Krishna is pavitra he is the Supreme pure all of those purifying qualities of God are within his name. Ah! We can always be in the divine presence of the Lord, if we keep… like the lotus flower, if we keep our stem and our roots fixed in God’s name, so we should begin our day by fixing our consciousness in Krishna by chanting his name, by studying the scriptures, by keeping all of our senses very purely immersed in his loving service by performing some puja. Let our everyday began with this and this alone nothing else should be a priority, nothing else should be our duty in the morning except our Sadhana, our purification. And then after that let us take some nice prasad which is also very purifying to the body, the mind and the soul and then let us go to our daily activities. But like lotus flowers we may have to do our duties, but we do not enjoy we do not socially take part in the enjoyments of those who are not in devotion to God.

The contamination that takes place in our heart is not when we are doing duties in this world, whether we are businessman or so forth. The contamination takes place afterward when our social life is intermingling with people who are materialistic by nature. When we start to take part in the things that they enjoy. We can do anything as our duty but we do not take part in enjoying what they enjoy because as soon as you do that you become… your consciousness becomes like that materialistic, non-devotional, you become further further and further implicated in the bondage of illusion. We want to wake up, materialistic activity simply put us deeper and deeper in slump of ignorance. So therefore, after performing our duties we should be friend and our social life should be with those persons who will help to cultivate the devotional sentiments in our heart, help to purify our life this is very important. That is why it is said,

‘sādhu-saṅga’, ‘sādhu-saṅga’ — sarva-śāstre kaya

[Cc. Madhya 22.54]

[The verdict of all revealed scriptures is that by even a moment’s association with a pure devotee, one can attain all success.]

That the association of Sadhus is the most precious most valuable treasure that is given by God in the whole creation, when we associate with saintly persons naturally the quality of devotion grows, when we associate with Krishna through His devotees through His name, through His prasad, through His murti, through His books, through His words through that association naturally Pure devotion awakens within our heart.

Test of your sincerity, in summary, how to become a pure devotee

So, to answer your question how to become a pure devotee this is the process. In summary, first of all we have to hear in the association of pure devotees. Through the process of hearing as I explained before faith awakens within our heart and then that faith must be nourished, it must be nourished by watering it with the holy name of God, with the studying of the scriptures it must be watered by associating with God and His devotees and it must be protected by following the principles of devotion. It is very simple; it is not a mystical secret. God helps those who help themselves. And if you simply follow this process of hearing, chanting, engaging in the service of the Lord and inquiring submissively regularly in the association of devotees, if you simply follow this very, very simple principles Krishna guarantees that by His divine arrangement pure devotion will be awaken within your heart,

man-manā bhava mad-bhakto

mad-yājī māṁ namaskuru

mām evaiṣyasi satyaṁ te

pratijāne priyo ’si me

[Bg. 18.65]

[Always think of Me, become My devotee, worship Me and offer your homage unto Me. Thus you will come to Me without fail. I promise you this because you are My very dear friend.]

Krishna says, “Always think of me, become my devotee, worship me and offer your homage unto me. In this way you will come to me without fail. This is my promise because you are my dear friend.” This process of Krishna consciousness is one hundred percent infallible! It is the…ordain is the promise of God. If you simply follow this process of always thinking of Krishna, becoming His devotee, offering your homage unto Him, you will come to Him without fail.
