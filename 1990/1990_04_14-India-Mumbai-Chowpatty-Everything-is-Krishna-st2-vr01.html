[Bg. 2.44]

[In the minds of those who are too attached to sense enjoyment and material opulence, and who are bewildered by such things, the resolute determination for devotional service to the Supreme Lord does not take place.]

‘To those who are too much attached to sense enjoyment and material opulence the resolute determination for devotional service to the lord cannot take place’.

True goal of life

Sometimes it is said, that sense gratification in Krishna Consciousness go ill together. The goal of life must be understood. This is the greatest misfortune in human society, that despite so much sophisticated processes of education and training, people are not trained to understand what is the goal of life and how to achieve it. In fact, people are trained, how to pursue those aspects of life which completely diverts one’s attention away from the true goal. The true goal of life is to repose all one’s affection in God and the greatest diversion is to repose our affection in anything else.

Real responsibility

The mother of Pandavas Queen Kunti, she prayed, “Dear Lord Sri Krishna please severe my attraction and all my attachments to my family as well as the whole dynasty of my husband’s family. Please severe all my attachments to both the Vrishnis and the Pandavas and let my attraction be only reposed in you.” Now people may think that Queen Kunti was hard-hearted woman, how could anyone want to break one’s affection to her own family members? But the fact is Queen Kunti was the best of all mothers. How is that possible? Because what she was praying, is that the lord severe all her illusory connections. She prayed, ‘‘Lord, as the Ganges forever flows to the sea without hindrance, let my attraction be forever drawn unto you without being diverted to anyone else. When all of your affection and all of your attractions and all of your attachments are directly connected to Krishna, united with Krishna, then and only then are you worthy or being a true mother, father, sister, brother, or friend. Until then you are unworthy because you do not know how to love and you do not know how to be loved.

Love begins when you love God. When you learn how to repose all your affection in Krishna, then you will be able to perceive, that your child is part of Krishna and then you will care for your child, body, mind and soul with such love and such attention, and such devotion, you will be the greatest mother, the greatest father, the greatest friend. But as long as we are thinking in terms of the physical body and we neglect to understand, how every living being is part of God, then your relationship is simply superficial and in illusion. After all, ah, we have so many mothers and so many fathers here tonight, so your child wears clothes, correct? So is it your duty to wash the child’s clothes? What do you think? Are you afraid to admit that you wash your children’s clothes or you have somebody wash it for you? Even if you have somebody else wash it, at least you have it washed, right, you have a washer man.

These are rich people tonight, they all have washer man. Where I am from, we wash our own clothes. So you have to children’s clothes washed, that is your responsibility. Cleanliness is next to the godliness but let me ask you another question? Do you feed your children or is washing their clothes enough? What do you think? Ah? When you wash children’s clothes, is that enough, bas, now my job is taken care, no more responsibilities.

We simply have to get to know him, ah, but in order to get to know him, we must want to know him, we must be eager to know him. In fact it is said, that the only price of entrance into the kingdom of God is eagerness to serve God, eagerness to know God, and eagerness to love God. Sometimes we are very dull, even we come to spiritual life. The lecture is going on and we are thinking, ah, very nice and I’ve heard it before. If you know one pastime, if you love Krishna, you will be just wanting to hear that one pastime for the rest of your life, ah?
