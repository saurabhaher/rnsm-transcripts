1990-057_Association_Of_Devotees_BG-10_09_Mumbai_-_27-07-90[1]

mac-cittā mad-gata-prāṇā
bodhayantaḥ parasparam
kathayantaś ca māḿ nityaḿ
tuṣyanti ca ramanti ca

The devotees of the Lord, Krishna explains, derive great satisfaction and bliss discussing topics of Me worshipping Me, finding pleasure in Me alone.

Durlabha Manav janm Lobhiya Sansaare

Durlabha Manav Janm Satsange tarava ae bhava Sindhu re

Human form of life is most rare, and the most and the most precious & valuable than all benedictions in this human form of life is to have the association of persons who are dedicating their lives in the loving service of the Supreme Personality Of Godhead. To not take full opportunity when it comes in the association of devotees is likened to neglecting/ mishandling the most precious jewel. If u r given a which is most valuable, you will be constantly conscious of caring for it, knowing that if you are neglectful, it will be stolen away. When people have large sums of money, they put it in the most secure place they can possibly find, knowing that in this world it is very likely that due to neglect, it will be lost or stolen. But what is the worth of money, people work very hard for money and then when they gain it, they take so many measures to protect the security of that money, can money bring u happiness, the answer is NO, can money bring u pace of mind, the answer is NO, the wealthy people of this world, do they have peace of mind? Can money stop death from knocking upon your door, the answer is NO. so what is the worth of money? it can bring LITTLE FOOD, it can bring social security, but IT CANNOT GIVE U happiness, it cannot give you peace, it cannot give you relief from old age, disease and death, and how much we are working day n night simply for money and how much protective we are of our money how much we scrutinize every aspect of our life to best utilize our money which has such nominal minimal and temporary value. How many people do not agree with me raise your hand. How many people agree! The more money you have the more you agree, Right, the less more money you have you want to try it and then give me your opinion. That is the great illusion. But what is the value of a moment association with devotees

Sri Chaitanya mahaprabhu has said

Sadhu Sang Sadhu Sang sarva shaastra kohe

Lahu maatra Saddhu sang, sarva Siddhi hoye

That a moment’s association of sadhus is so valuable that even 1 moment of such association can open the doors to eternal liberation

Prahlad maharaj in the 7thcanto of Bhagavat Puraana describes that if you want relief from repetition of birth n death if u want eternal peace and happiness of being eternally situated at the feet of God, there is only 1 way, you must take the dust from the feet of the devotee of the Lord, u must roll in that dust and smear it on every part of your body, you must raise your hands and chant Hari Hari Bol, if u r not willing to do that, you are not qualified for the peace of the spiritual world. What does it mean to roll in the dust of the feet of the devotee, it means to becomes their servants. Feet represents service, just like Lakshmi devi, she is always at the feet of Vishnu, what is she doing there, she is massaging His feet as a humble obedient servant, that is called Paada sevanam, the lotus feet of K are the supreme reservoir of all pleasure. So much so that Krishna Himself aspires to taste the dust of His lotus feet. When Markandeya muni was floating in the ocean of waves of devastation through many many millenniums through storms and winds, cold and heat alas he found great relief when he saw an island at the end of the ocean and on that island he saw a banyan tree floating close by in the waters and floating on the boat of that banyan tree leaf was the child Mukunda who had his toes in his mouth. Why God takes form of a baby to suck upon his toes. Because babies naturally suck upon their toes , it is not in appropriate and Lord Sri Krishna was aspiring to understand that what is it about my feet that even the greatest personalities surrender in the entire creation surrender their lives to get a drop of the taste of My feet, I want to taste it myself. Bu then Krishna explains,

sādhavo hṛdayaṁ mahyaṁ

sādhūnāṁ hṛdayaṁ tv aham

mad-anyat te na jānanti

nāhaṁ tebhyo manāg api

that the feet of my devotes are even more worshippable than my feet, that is the conclusion. Those great souls who have surrendered their lives at the lotus feet of the Lord, their lotus feet are considered to be boats which carry us across ocean of Birth and Death. So to associate with such persons is the most valuable and precious thing in this entire human life… within all existence. We work how , how many hours a day do we work for money, 8 – 9 hours , how many hours are we spending our money, how many hours are we protecting our money or resources which are depending on this money and we already discussed, what is the value of money. it cannot give you happiness, it cannot give you peace, it cannot protect you from the repetition of birth, old age disease and death, but the association of saintly persons that alone can bring u eternal peace,, everlasting happiness and carry you across the ocean of birth old age dis and death and how much are we attentive to protect and care for this valuable jewel that we have been given? Most people simply don’t have time because they r too busy with their money with matters ,right… they are simply too busy seeking after broken pieces of glass to take care of the rare diamond. Acc to SB, this is called misfortune, you are given the ultimate fortune of life, but how much are we are really really taking care of that how much r we really meditative and conscious of the value of the association of such sadhus. And who is a Sadhu? Sri Chaitany mahaprabhu is asked how do I know who is a genuine devotee of Krishna He says anyone who chants my name is a devotee. How much are we indebted to Krishna for one another..

Maharaj Prataprudra was the king for all Orissa , he had a palace, queen and so many nice children, he had a great armies. He was the most powerful King of his time. During his time in India, the great conquerors of the Mughal rule they were defeating practically every city, town and land in the Bharat varsha/ India, but there were few places where the Kings were so powerful that they could not conquer, even the Muslim conquerors Aurangzeb, Babri—they were afraid of the King of Jaipur and the Nawaab Hussain Shah he conquered all of Bengal and brought it under his rule and then he took all his armies and personally led them to Orissa, but when he met the powerful soldiers of Maharaj Prataprudra he was defeated and had to run back to Bengal. He was one of the greatest Kings of all times but he understood that his life only had meaning so long as he could attain the mercy of the lord, otherwise all of his money , all his Kingdom everything was a useless waste. All his family attachments simply a disturbance if they were not engaged in God’s service. So he went to his guru and asked him, I have heard that Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu is Krishna Himself, please grant me an audience with him. In the kingdom of Orissa, every citizen wanted an audience with the King, doesn’t everybody wants to meet a great man, even if u don’t believe in what he says, u still want to meet him just because he is famous. Right. Just like in America, George Bush is The President, most of the people in America do not like him, do not think he is doing a good job, but how much they would all be very honored to meet him. “Oh… I had breakfast with George Bush, Oh Really!! You had breakfast with George Bush!! Unbelievable, fantastic, Wonderful Yes Yes, So I have had breakfast with George Bush, I don’t believe in him, I don’t like what he is doing but he is famous, so I had breakfast with him, therefore I am very happy. Everyone likes to meet famous people. I remember some very very great Sadhus from India , they went to EU, I know one very big man, he got his pictures taken of shaking hands with Adolf Hitler, and in his ashram today he has a big blown up picture of shaking hands with Adolf Hitler and every says Oh you’ve met Hitler!! Yes Yes , I have met Hitler and he thought my philosophy was very good. How many people like Hitler? Nobody likes Hitler, But because he was so famous if u met him everyone would say Oh he met Mr. Hitler, Oh really u did what did he say, what did he do? Isint that true. The potency of famous is so attractive that everyone wants to be with or to meet or to say that I was with the famous person. Maharaj Prataprudra was so famous in all the land, he was The King and everyone wanted his audience. But he was thinking, “ if only I could have the audience of Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, then my life will be of some meaning, otherwise what is the use of all this?

Na Dhanam Na Janam Na Sundarim

Kavita va Jagadish KAmaye

Mama Janmani Janmani Ishware

Bhavatad Bhakti Ahituki tvaayi

I don’t want money, I do not want beautiful women, I do nt want fame and recognition amongst me, I do not even want liberation. Birth after birth I simply want your service My Lord. So Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu would not meet this King. He would not meet him because by nature a King is so much involved in worldly activities that he felt it was below His integrity to meet such a person.

” nishkinchanasya bhagavad-bhajanonmukhasya”

Lord Chaitanya said for a person who is desiring to cross over the ocean of birth and death, to intimately associate with people involved in sense gratification is more deadly than drinking poison willingly. He meant this literally. If someone was to offer you poison, you would say Ahh that’s poison, but when you intimately associate with sinful people it is more deadly than drinking poison because poison can only temporarily finish one little bodily incarnation, but the association with sinful people can destroy cause death to all your Spiritual propensities. So Mahaprabhu would not meet him and the King was thinking , “ He would not meet me because I am a King, then what is the use of this damn Kingdom. I will give up my Kingdom, I will give up my family, I will give up my palace, I will wear the bark from trees, I will mat my hair, I will live in the jungles like a mad man. Today I will do all this and for the rest of my life if that is what is required to get the mercy of Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu. He wasn’t saying all this politically, he was about to do it , then the devotes realized “ My God, he is such a good king and such a great devotee just be patient the Lord will be kind upon u, just be patient please please please. He was given the blessings of all the great souls. They saw his firm determination. Then the glorious ceremony of rath yatra took place in Shri Purushottam Puri Dham and Jagannath was sitting upon His cart. Jagannath means the Lord , The King of all of The Universe. We are little kings but He is The Supreme King. The husband is the king of his little family and he is the king “Ahh my home, my kingdom” Right… But secretly his wife is thinking that I am the queen and everyone knows the king is ruled by the queen. All women know this, so secretly men have never caught unto . usually you have to take Sanyaas to be able to see it in truth. So everyone is thinking – they are the king, they are the queen, they are the rulers. I am the king of my company, I am the king of the country. But Jagannath is the King of the Universe and Maharaj Prataprudra understood that with all my hundreds and thousands and millions of soldiers, with all my millions and millions of subjects, I am nothing but the menial servant of Lord Jagannath. He took a broom and accepted the position of a street sweeper and began to sweep the street before Jagannath ji and that activity , not only was he sweeping the street before Lord Jagannath but he was sweeping the road so that all the devotees of Lord Jagannath could dance without the obstruction of stones and pebbles. So in this way he was voluntarily taking position of the servant of the feet of every devotee and the Lord Himself. He was meditating on the feet of every devotee of Jagannath. “Let their feet not be inconvenienced by the stones and the pebbles and the dirt on the road, let them dance nicely” and let Jagannath see with His lotus eyes a clean and beautiful street. When Lord Gaurasundar saw this humble approach to the feet of His devotees and The Lord, His heart melted and shortly The Lord not only gave him His audience but also embraced him again and again and again and again and again and bestowed upon him His ultimate mercy- Prema Bhakti, Divine love, Eternal liberation, Eternal peace, Eternal Happiness. He did not even have to give up his kingdom and be a mendicant. He continues his service to The Lord. Why was he given that benediction? Why was he given the highest goal of life – Pure devotional service? And still he was allowed to keep his family, to keep his palace, to keep his kingdom. Because he proved to The Lord that in his heart, there was nothing so valuable, so meaningful, so precious in life than the association and blessings of The Lord and His devotees. He showed by his own life, his own example that he was willing to give up everything for that no …. Before that nothing has any value. As the amount of light of a firefly is compared to the light of the sun that is the difference between everything that we can possibly acquire in this world and the value of the association of even one moment with a great soul or with great souls.

Therefore Krishna says , “ My devotees, their greatest happiness is to come together to chant together My glories” and Lord Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu who is God Himself, taking the form of a devotee to show us what it means to be a devotee, where did He find His pleasure

Harer nama harer nama harer namaiva kevalam
kalau nasty eva nasty eva nasty eva gatir anyatha:

He found His joy, His bliss in bringing the devotees together raising their arms and chanting “ Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare

Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare

People think what are these people doing middle of the night, dancing around and chanting.!! Sometimes we think, What will my neighbours think!!! AHHH, I will chant and dance in someone else’s neighbourhood , but not my neighbourhood, because what will they think of me!! They will think I have gone crazy, even worse than that, they will think that I have become a Hare Krishna. They will think I am too much religious. After all religion means do a little pooja and some days you give some donation or charity and besides that you are a normal human being. Hannn..” What do you think Tattva, Not like that ?” because the real normal position of a human life is to remember Krishna Twenty four hours a day, that is normal for the soul. To come together to chant the Holy names, to share each others hearts for the glorification of The Lord. Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu has taught us that this is the most valuable, most precious and most worthy endeavour in life. To engage in the service of the great souls, to hear from them, to dance and chant with them. There is nothing so precious as this, there will never be and there never was. SO let us calculate how many hours a day are we thinking of earning and protecting money and how many hours a day are we thinking of association with the Vaishnavas in protecting that in nourishing that. To the degree we take this principal seriously to that degree we become spiritually advanced. To the degree we neglect this, to that degree we remain as we are. Off course if we like it the way we are then all power to you my friend until you grow old, get diseased and die and then you regret , What have I done with my life!!! Do you know the last thing what Lord Jesus Christ did before he left this world? The last example he gave to his devotees and to the world, they were all sitting for dinner, its called ‘The Last Supper’ and Lord Jesus, he rolled up his robes, he put a towel around himself, he got a bowl of scented water and he went to each of his devotees to wash their feet and the 1st devotee Peter said I cannot allow you to do all this, You are my master, You are God, how can I allow you to touch my dirty feet, what to speak of washing them. Lord Jesus Christ said, No No, if I am your Master, you must obey. I must show you by my example that I am the servant of the feet of all my devotees and if you want to be my servant you have to follow my path and be the servant of the lotus feet of all my devotees and then he washed each of their feet. DO you know how painful it was to let their master, their Guru, The Lord of their life wash their feet. As the water was cascading along their feet, they were thinking My God!! This is to the extent that my Guru wants me to be the servant of his devotees. After he finished his feet washing ceremony, he said I have 1 last commandment to give all of You. To Love one another as I love You. You must see how much I love each and every one of you and if you want to be my servant, you must love one another the same way, I am loving you. That is what we mean when we , associate with devotees. To develop that atmosphere, the atmosphere of love and trust, the atmosphere of seeking out the nectar from each devotee and every devotee has nectar because Krishna is in everyone’s heart. Every devotee has something utterly unique to contribute to the association of devotees. Whoever we are, we must see that unique quality in every devotee. Even if the person is sometimes very obnoxious and intolerable and we just don’t like him in regards to our false ego sometimes this happens. But we must seek out, we must seek out search for that unique quality in every devotee that he has to contribute to everyone else and everyone has that quality if we look for it. We should love that quality, we should become his servant or her servant. This is how we take advantage, this is how we fully grasp the opportunity at hand.

When Krishna played upon His flute, the Gopis they left their parents, they left everything to join Krishna. Well I will tell you my dear friends whenever the kirtan of the Holy name is going on in the association of saintly persons, the flute of Krishna is playing. How many of us are in the mood of the Gopis and are willing to risk everything, lose everything, to run to Khar station to join the Kirtan. That is the qualification to understand the value. On Sunday Krishna is playing His flute, not only is He playing His flute all day long at Radha Gopinath Mandir, but His flute is resounding in the sound of His name as all the devotees are dancing and chanting His name as His glories are being discussed in Srimad Bhagavatam and Bhagavad Gita discourse. Are we in the mood of the gopis? Nothing has any value except that, in comparison to that. The Gopis renounced everything and attained the highest position of Krishna’s true blessing and did they lose their families? They did not anything. Rupa Goswami says “do not think if you surrender to Krishna you will be the loser, you will not lose anything except bondage, anxiety, frustration and suffering – that’s all you’ll lose. And what will you gain? The Ultimate Goal – Krishna consciousness!!

Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare

Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare

Ans we see what happened is, before Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu directly embraced Prataprudra maharaj, he first wanted to give the world another example. Prataprudra Maharaj he was feeling so bereft, this was immediately after he took his vow to give up everything. The devotees were thinking, we must do something to keep him from retiring from the kingdom, we need him as King. So they convinced him send your sons to see the Lord. So when his sons came before Lord Chaitanya, when Lord Chaitanya saw them he saw that they had beautiful lotus eyes and a very dark complexion and his son reminded Him of Krishna, then Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu thinking that this boy is reminding me of Krishna and there is no one so dear to me, no one to beloved to me as the person who reminds Me of Krishna that He embraced the boy again and again and again. The news came to Maharaj Prataprudra of the special mercy that came upon his son and when the son returned home, Maharaj Prataprudra with tears in his eyes embraced the son and because the son had just been embraced by Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, he felt the very embrace of The Lord upon his body when he embraced his son. So what is the lesson to be learnt from this story?

The lesson is that it was by the arrangement of the devotees that his son got this blessing. It is only by the arrangement of the great souls that we can get the embrace of Krishna, otherwise it is impossible. And also the King he experienced in full the divine mercy of The Lord by coming in divine contact with someone who had been embraced by God. SO similarly if we come in contact with someone who is near and dear to Krishna, through that person we can actually feel the embrace of Krishna directly.

Ans- You just contradicted yourself, first you said its impossible then you said it is very difficult. It may be very difficult.

By the mercy of Guru even if you are a lame man, you can cross over the mountains, even if you are a blind man you can see the stars in the sky, even if you are a dumb man you can speak beautiful poetry. For one who gains the mercy of Guru, nothing is impossible. For one who is touched by the mercy of Guru we can see Krishna in everyone’s heart, at least theoretically. We have to act according to our intelligence and not according to our emotions. Our emotions may think, this man is despicable even though he is in the garb of a devotee. There is nothing about him that I can tolerate I simply want to avoid him at all costs. After all I am a great Vaishnava and he is below my integrity to associate with. Do you know how St Francis of Assissi attained liberation/ enlightenment. You have heard of St Francis, he is a great sage in the Christian tradition. It is described that there were many stages in his life that brought him to the point of surrendering to God. But in his own narrations one of the three most important stepping stones to his enlightenment in salvation to the service of God is a very very interesting experience. He was the son of a very very wealthy cloth merchant. Does that sound familiar!!! He was the biggest cloth merchant in all of Assissi and his father wanted to wanted to turn everything over to him. Sounds familiar. In that village on the outskirts was a leprosy colony because in those days leprosy was a very bad disease in Europe and they would not allow people with leprosy to associate ordinary people of the society, because it was considered very contagious they would make them live in caves or they would send them to islands. There would just make a very very minimal existence type of building and make them live like cattle. They were most misused, misunderstood and the most untolerated people in all of society. From the time of St Francis’ birth he had an absolute fear of leprosy and anyone with leprosy made him so disgusting he couldn’t even look at that person the body was full of pus , sores and deformation and the misery, suffering dirtiness , contagiousness. It was in his heart that so much was just intolerable that he couldn’t even look at the face of a leper. When a leper came and begged something from him he would immediately practically run away” Horrible, go away from here you filthy thing” One day he was riding on a beautiful white horse, he became A Knight, he was decorated in very shiny beautiful armour, he was in a very prestigious and recognised position within the society, a leper came out to him and pulled out his diseased hands and begged for donation. But then St Francis, something came over him, he took the leper’s hands in his hands. He put them to his mouth and kissed them with great affection and he took all the money that his father had given him and gave it to that leper. At that point he actually felt love for that leper and that act pleased the Lord so much that he became enlightened. That austerity was so dear to the Lord, that it was qualification to become a saint. So the more intolerable a person appears to you, specially a devotee, the more the Lord is giving you an opportunity to become enlightened by tolerating him, by being his servant. Anyone can serve someone you like, but how many be the servant of someone you don’t like out of love for God, out of love for your Guru. That is the test of your sincerity, that is called tapasya or austerity. It is not impossible, it is very difficult. But the more difficult it is, the more credit you get if you do it. This is the test of our sincerity. Can you do it ? With the grace of Guru and Krishna you can do it. Raise your hands if you think you can do it with the grace of Guru and Krishna. On your own you cannot. Sanatana Goswami had oozing sores all over his body and Lord Chaitanya would embrace him every day with pus and blood and everything. Sanatana said,” why are you doing this? Its horrible smelling, nobody wants to get near me. Lord Chaitanya said that “ I see all this pus and blood as nectar oozing from your lotus like body.
