Lecture Transcription

Krishna Consciousness is a Change of Heart

With everyone taking seriously their internal purification, because Krishna consciousness is a change of heart. The external forms are essentially only worthwhile in so much as the change of heart takes place. Giving a nice lecture, Ronald Reagan can do that, all he has to do is study our books for few hours. Leading a nice kirtan, Frank Sinatra can do that. I don’t know, you ever heard of him. Anoop Jalota can crowd in thousands. This is not what is going to attract people to our movement.

This movement is based on the principle of love and trust. And unless there is love and trust amongst each other, sensitive, intelligent people will not take our philosophy, nor they will take our cultural presentations very seriously. As it is described by Srila Prabhuapda, “It is like an empty husk, without any rice.” It appears like rice but there is nothing inside. Chaitanya Mahaprabhu’s mission is a mission of compassion. Lord Nityananda Prabhu demonstrated by his own life’s example, the perfection of forgiveness and the perfection of extending and sacrificing of oneself to help uplift another. And the great acharyas in our parampara, they have given their life to this principle alone.

There is a famous saying in English -Charity begins at home. Unless we are willing to treat each other with forgiveness, respect, compassion and love, there is no hope of dealing with the karmis, on any Krishna Conscious level that has any substance. So to strengthen the integrity of our own spiritual community from within, is most essential in our developing this.

Devotees need to develop Vaikunta Consciousness

What does this entail? Krishna conscious love is not just a sentimental concept. Love means expressing humility and service, by putting ourselves last and putting all others first. Of course, for a neophyte devotee this is very difficult. But this is called austerity, tapasya. This is the price we must pay. We can show love for one another by putting ourselves last and all other first, to engage in the service of the vaishnavas. Love doesn’t mean simply exchanging flowers saying, “I love you” and then criticizing that person half way down the block. Love is expressed through service and the greatest service is to somehow or other help uplift each other so that we can become fully Krishna conscious. And when amongst each other we deal with one another in this respect and believe me thousands and thousands of sincere souls from all classes of the society will come to take shelter. Of this there is no doubt. Because everyone is looking and everyone one is searching for Vaikuntha. And this spirit is the definition of Vaikuntha.

Prabhupada said, “Vaikuntha consciousness is, everyone else is better than me, so I am your servant.” So if we really want to spread this movement, this is first. You can distribute a hundred million books, but who is going to take it seriously, if we are fighting amongst each other. We can give lectures to ten million people at a time, but who is going to take us seriously if we are still selfishly motivated. We can sing the most beautiful bhajans and kirtans, but as long as we are still contaminated with false pride, who is going to want.

So therefore, we want to develop a spiritual community with an integrity based on the highest philosophical principles, and the culmination of that philosophy is that we come together in the humble spirit of service to uplift one another to learn to love and trust one another. So many problems come into our life, but my opinion is, so what? Who cares? The only problem is forgetting Krishna. Krishna is big and everything else and everyone else is small. When you forget Krishna the problems of this world appear very big. But when you remember Krishna, they are utterly insignificant and that is the fact. The problems that we have amongst each other is the most little, petty, insignificant things that are not worth anything we take so seriously, that means no Krishna consciousness. And to the degree we are plagued with this concept of my problems, to that degree we become very selfish and we cannot extend ourselves to uplift our brothers and sisters. So when we chant the holy names of the lord and when we hear his transcendental glories we are meant to invoke within our heart a sense of understanding how great is Krishna and how glorious is Krishna. When we see how Krishna is dancing on Kaliya serpent gracefully we get some glimpse of how great is Krishna.

Krishna is Great

In India I was recently reading, the major single cause of death is snake bites. People are very frightful of little snakes. There are some tiny little snakes crawling in the dirt, we become very afraid. Kaliya had hundreds of heads. And he was gigantic and there was fire, his poison was so strong that it was creating fire, and Krishna was gracefully dancing upon him. This is Krishna. So when we understand how great is Krishna, we realise how insignificantly small minute we are, and how utterly ridiculous all of our problems are in this material world. and when we remember Krishna there are no more problems.

Sublime realisations of Hayagriva Maharaj

Recently, I was with Hayagriva Maharaj, and he was laying on his death bed. According to the doctors he only has two to three months to live. By God’s inconceivable arrangement he has cancer that has spread throughout his entire body. And of course, he cannot walk. He is just lying in the bed twenty-four hours a day. So just before I left New Vrindavan, I was sitting with him. And how Krishna conscious this man, is the most beautiful sight for the eyes to see. There were tears in his eyes. And he spoke to me. He said, “You know Radhanath Maharaj,” these words were coming from the core from his heart, “I used to think that I was something very great. I used to think that I have the world in my hands. At one time I thought I was a big devotee. I was the president of New Vrindavan, I was the GBC, I was the first person to know Prabhupada, in the entire International society for Krishna consciousness movement. And later on I fell into various degraded activities and I was thinking, I can do what I want, when I want. But now look at me, I am lying in this bed, I cannot walk, I need two people to just to move my body, this deadly disease of cancer is spreading to every cell of my body and there is nothing I can do about it. Within a month or two, I will die. I am in an utterly helpless, hopeless condition of life. I have no control over anything anymore. In this condition all there is Krishna. I am nothing…. I have nothing, except Krishna.” And he began to cry. And he said, “Srila Prabhupada, is so merciful, he has tolerated the most grievous offences that I have committed, he has patiently waited for me, though most sinful activities I have committed. Now when I need him most, he has come to give me the perfection of life.” As he was breaking down crying, he was chanting the holy names of Krishna. Saying, “All that I am have, is Krishna. I am helpless. Whatever Krishna wants to do with me, that is his right, that is his eternal right”.

So, I began to reflect and I spoke to Hayagriva Maharaj that, Maharaj you know everyone in this material world is in the exact same position as you just spoke of. We are all completely helpless. We are all completely hopeless. We are all totally completely dependent on mercy of Krishna. The only difference is you are aware of it and nobody else is. We are still under the illusion that we are in control. And then he looked up at me he said,” I have nobody but Krishna” and then he asked me, “Is there anyone else to turn to except Krishna? Is Krishna God? He said if Krishna is not God, that means all the great saints, all the great devotees and acharyas from all the religions in the world, they are all liars. If Krishna is not the god that means all the scriptures of the world are wrong. Is this possible?” then he smiled and he said, “It is impossible. Krishna is God”.

Devotee needs to be Sincere

So Krishna puts his intimate associates in these condition of life as a lesson to all of us. and if we are sincere, we will very personally take these lessons to the heart. As that guest of New Vrindavan explaining there is nothing more convincing than the love, the concern and the attitude of service that is shared amongst the devotees. And I sincerely and honestly believe that it is this principle, that we must meditate upon, reflect upon and we must make internal vows of how we can be part of a solution, not a part of the problem. By chanting together, dancing together, hearing Krishna conscious philosophy together and serving one another with respect for every living beings spiritual integrity, that means to be compassionate, patient and kind this is how we will attract the world that the lotus feet of Sri Sri Radha Gopinath. So I am so happy to be amongst all of you again today, I am your servant.

Hare Krishna
