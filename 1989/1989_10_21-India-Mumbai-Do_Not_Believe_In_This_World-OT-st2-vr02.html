Alcoholism In America

I would like to thank all of you for coming this afternoon. Especially, I would like thank Nikki for the fine arrangement today. The alcoholics anonymous organization is [00:00:26 - 00:00:30 inaudible] many places America. [00:00:32 - 00:00:38 inaudible]. Spoken many times and I have found perhaps the most receptive audience in the whole organization. [00:00:47 - 00:00:51 inaudible]. Being a minister, I lecture in many churches, many temples. I have lectured often in assemblies of Jews, Christians, Muslims, Hindus, Buddhists, atheists, scholars and I found that in most religions of the world the people approach the religion in a very superficial, in a very ritualistic manner. The conception of God is more something that has been accepted due to tradition rather than due to a very deeply ingrained to [00:1:48 - 00:01:52 inaudible] and therefore most people they chant their mantras, they do their puja, they give their donations more as an act of routine.

Alcoholics anonymous is a body of people who have come together not due to some family tradition, but they have come together because they feel from the core of their hearts that they are in utter need, they are in [00:2:030 - 00:02:32 inaudible], they are in desperation, without the help of God, they are all [00:02:35 - 00:02:38 inaudible]. and reading some of the literature like the alcoholics anonymous institutions I found that this is the basic principle. When we are helpless, we are hopeless, we are totally dependent on the mercy of God and without his mercy we have no hope.

Alcoholism is one of the great weapons of this material energy to keep men and women intoxicated and completely lose scope of the real purpose of life. This energy is so powerful and we are so [00:03:31 - 00:03:33 inaudible]. I think most of you can fully grasp what I'm trying to say. The alcoholic disease is so overpowered, so overwhelming to the mind, to the senses, to every aspect of our life that it makes a monster on [00:03:59 - 00:04:01 inaudible] person. I know my own brother due to addiction, very simple, very humble, very innocent young boy, he became like a monster. He was stealing my mother's own wearing jewellery and selling it, just to get money for his drugs and he knew that he was doing the most horrible thing but he couldn't stop. In America alcoholism, like anything else in America, is very advanced. America is the country of the world that everyone is chasing after. It’s technology, it’s Scientific development, it’s economic policies, it’s industrialization but along with it alcoholism, drug addiction, [00:05:13 - 00:05:15 inaudible] and so many other social frustrations.

Alcoholism, Deaths, Divorce, Juvenile Delinquency In America

In the United States of America, the number one cause of death is Alcoholism. The number one cause of death is automobile accidents and 80% of all automobile accidents, there are serious involving serious injuries and death are due to alcohol, drunk and driving. Ahh.. The divorce rate in America is for every two marriages within five years, the divorce rate is sixty-six percent and according to statistics one of the major reasons for divorce is drugs and alcohol. It destroys the family unity. Ahh…This leads to another problem: juvenile delinquency. It is one of the greatest problems in America today. They do not know what to do? In the city streets juvenile delinquency is so bad, is so horrible that most of the governments, they tried so many welfare approaches, so many agencies, so many outreaches, most of the leaders have thrown up their hands and said there is nothing we can do about this juvenile delinquency. It’s just getting worse and worse and worse and worse, to the extent there were people can’t even walk outside their door at night in cities of America without fearing being rouged, killed or raped. And it is mostly by the youths. Ninety percent of juvenile crimes all in America is by changing broken homes, children whose parents were divorced usually due to drinking and drugs and those children practically all of them are engaged in alcohol and drugs. What I am telling you is alcohol and drugs create the greatest material conflict in the world. The United State of America is being destroyed inside out due to this horrible disease which is making monsters out of human beings.

Only God Can Help

I was explaining that it is very difficult to give up. In fact, there is an example that the river flows very strong and the most powerful of all living creatures under physiology. When the elephant goes to bathe in the river, when the current is strong, even the most powerful elephant despite all strength is washed away. What to speak of the ordinary man? Contrary to the strength of the elephant, the most insignificant of all creatures is the tiny little fish ahh…. Now the fish in the same river easily swims downstream upstream across the stream. No problem. How is that possible? Because the elephant is trying to go against the current of the river. With all his strength the river [00:09:36 - 00:09:38 inaudible], by its own individual ability but the tiny little fish has learned how to take shelter of the river. Therefore, is free, is independent to go with anyway he likes. So similarly this material nature is very powerful. It is undefeatable and the sins and vices that we become habituated to are unconquerable and whether we have hundreds of millions of rupees, whether we have high, high influential position within society. We see such a thing is alcoholism, completely controls us. We have no power to defend.

But one who knows how to take shelter of the lord, there is a source of everything that exists. On the basis of that power, that strength of God, one is liberated. One is no longer under the influence of vices in this world.

The twelve steps of alcoholics anonymous is one of the most spiritual facts that read about because it is teaching people, how to genuinely from the core of one's heart accept and admit that we are powerless and that without the help of God we are hopeless. The first step we admitted we were powerless over alcohol then our lives have become the [00.11.55 inaudible] came to believe that a Power greater than ourselves can restore to (00:11:58 - 00:12:02 inaudible) may it decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of God as we [00:12:07 - 00:12:08 inaudible]. It’s very hard to find one person in one church, synagogue or mosque or temple who has truly decided to turn their life to over to God. Ahh….and that is the purpose of life and that is goal of life according to all the great religious traditions of the world.

So as I have said so many times before, I am very grateful to what this wonderful fellowship is doing in humanity. Recently I met one doctor who was an alcoholic just a few years back. Our Nikki, he was his counsellor. Nikki told me this person was practically a hopeless, impossible case, very difficult. Now this man is dedicating his life internally and externally for the service of God, not only still a doctor but he's truly a deeply religious man, truly calling out the mercy of God. He told me if it wasn't for his alcoholism he would have been like every other successful doctor in society, in complete ignorance of the goal of life, completely callous to the truth that we are completely dependent on mercy of God.

Factually without the mercy of God, you could not breathe one breath because he is supplying the air. He is supplying the sunshine, the rain. He is supplying the earth in which the food grows. Knowledge is to understand the truth and that truth is that one hundred percent of every aspect of our life is completely dependent on the mercy of God but the common man is thinking that I am doing very well, I am the controller and everything is going according to my plans. This is the man’s illusion. In America, the wealthiest people are the most frustrated. They must take sleeping pills to sleep at night. They must go to psychiatrists just to be able to deal with their daily activities and most of them are drinking and taking drugs in complete illusion.

The beginning of knowledge is to understand the truth that I am completely dependent on the mercy of God at all times. Alcohol might be an addiction and intoxicant but this whole world is intoxicated, intoxicated by ignorance and illusion. Therefore, this doctor told me that he considers it the great mercy of God that he was put into this situation a desperate hopeless case of alcoholism. His family was turning against him. He couldn't do his profession right. His whole life was totally in shambles and in this condition he realized his position. In this position he came into the fellowship of Alcoholics Anonymous organization and was pulled to humble himself, to put aside his pride and admit with all honesty that I need help and only the help of God is the power to help me and how through all of my brothers and sisters, the help of God is coming to me. He was telling me what a wonderful benediction it was, to become an alcoholic and to come into the congregation of such people, otherwise he said my life would be wasted like practically everyone else in this world.

Atheist King Constantine Turns To God For Help

We find that most genuine religious people throughout history had to be put in a crisis situation beyond their power to solve. If they are really genuine, will turn to God. Even the Roman Empire, the Romans hated the Christians, you know this? The Romans were the worst enemy of the Christians. Under the king of Rome, Christians would be kidnapped, imprisoned and there would be public exhibitions where they would be fed to Lions and the Colosseum in Rome [00:17:50 - 00:17:54 inaudible] where Christians were forced to kill each other and tens of thousands of people would be there to watch and cheer up and to make the best. Like the horse races? Ahh… but the king of Rome, he was invalid and his army was being completely defeated and he had no one to turn to and nowhere else to turn to and he could understand that I’m going lose everything and I'm going to die. He was an atheist and he thought, my God, nothing I can do to solve this situation. Their all power was being destroyed. Maybe there's a God. Then he thought of God. He said my dear Lord I am hopeless, I am helpless, I need you. Please help. And when he desperately, hopelessly turned to God. He had a vision that I will help you win this war and then you have to become my friend. [00:19:10 -00:19:11 inaudible]. Then he became victorious. This was King Constantine and then he officially made the whole Roman Empire Christian. So tens and thousands, billions of examples.

Avantidesh Brahmin’s Realization After Helplessness

In the Srimad Bhagavatam, there is a story of a Brahmin from Avantidesh. Very wealthy businessman great power, influence and money and he was a very cruel man because he was thinking that I have my life under control. I am better than everybody else. See how much I have, see what I can do. But then one day, beyond his control thieves came, and stole all of his property. He was left with nothing. In those days there was no insurance policies. And all of his friends, all these aristocratic people who simply used him because of his social position, they wanted to nothing to do with him. His wife left him, his children left him, he was lying on the ground with one set of clothes and he had nothing and no one in this world and he simply began to cry, his heart grievously, grievously weeping. Everything he was attached too, everything he loved, is gone. And in that condition after hours and hours and hours and hours of crying, pain and suffering, he had an awakening in his heart. He just turned to God. My life will be [00:21:26 - 00:31:27 inaudible] and with true honest devotion, in a helpless condition he cried out the name of God and then by crying out the name of God once, in helpless state of mind, one can experience full presence of God within himself and he became a completely liberated Saint at that moment and all the unlimited peace and happiness from within just began to pour from him and then with tears of joy, he began to praise the Lord and to this day thousands and thousands years later we read this man’s teaching and we are inspired by his example. So you see most of us in order to take religion seriously, God seriously, we have to be trampled upon. We have to realize our true position. Completely dependent on his mercy. Of course, one doesn't have to go through these things but usually that is what is needed.

You have heard the saying, ‘experience is the best teacher.’ How many of you have heard this saying? Raise your hand. Experience is the best teacher. Ahh…most of you. But do you know the whole saying? If you know the rest of the saying, raise your hand. Nobody? That's all we have? The saying is experience is the best teacher and a fool learns by no other way. Another words, an intelligent man just by hearing proper understanding of life he will act accordingly, but a fool after hearing repeatedly he will not listen he will not accept he will go against it and he will become smashed as a result and through that experience, then he will learn, the hard way, the school of hard knocks, Ahh... So all the great spiritual teachers. This organization is not sectarian by nature but we can accept and embrace the great teachings of all the great saints Lord Jesus Christ, Mohammed, Lord Sri Krishna, Lord Buddha. They have all taught that this world is a place of birth and death. This world is a place where ultimately everyone has to suffer, whoever you are, whatever your position is, but you are completely dependent on God and your real constitutional position. Your nature is to serve him, to love him and to call out for him in devotion. We have heard this again and again and again. Millions of people every day go to the religious foundations and the religious buildings and hear this message but practically no one takes it seriously in their life. They just go on with all their sinful ways, ahh…. They go on with all the materialistic ways and they pay their donation and they listen to the sermon and they say oh yes, yes very good I agree but in their hearts they do not agree.

You see, spiritual life is meant to change your heart, not to just entertain life or give you some sense, false sense of security. So very few people are willing to learn through hearing but when experience comes then we have really no road to turn. So one who is addicted to alcohol he only has two directions to turn. Either the Alcoholics Anonymous organization where they are teaching you how to depend on God and by his unlimited power free from this world of hate and the other direction is the slow horrible miserable death which will cost incredible suffering to all the friends, relatives and loved ones. Ahh… I think most of you in this room have come to that trust and are all very proud today that you accepted the right road, back to God. So consider the blessing whatever way you come to the conclusion that you understand helpless position upon God, accept his blessing. Never let go of that blessing. We need each other.

There is a verse in Srimad Bhagavatam, mahat-sevāṁ dvāram āhur vimuktes tamo-dvāraṁ yoṣitāṁ saṅgi-saṅgam [SB 5.5.2].

Just this morning I was, I was constantly [00:27:32 - 00:27:33 inaudible] and I quoted this verse and I used all of you as an example. The verse is by associating with godly people, we become godly and by associating with degraded people we become degraded and this is an absolute law and there's no exceptions. Show me any exception. And I explained to them that the first principle of Alcoholics Anonymous is you already have such a strong tendency for drinking; forget about your future life if you associate with anyone who drinks. Correct? This is what you teach? If you want to give it up, associate with people who is encouraging you on the right path, who is inspiring, giving you strength. This is the process of spiritual life. But when we lose God from the centre ahh… and then we are simply victim of our surroundings.

So my humble request and appeal to all of you today is to take advantage of what God has given you, in the association of one another. It is said in the Bible that a man should be the keeper of his brother ahh…. In fact we need each other and such persons like Gabri and Nikki there are many more amongst you but I know personally these two. Very, very exalted souls. They are dedicating their lives to helping to reach out to giving the gift of God that has been given to them ahh…. They are truly religious men. You see in giving we receive.

Draupadi’s Prideless State

Do not become proud. No one has anything to be proud of. But in this world everyone is proud, except most of you in this room. You have been smashed hard enough to know you have nothing to be proud of. And in that pride less condition you accepted help of God and turned to God. Just like you saw in Mahabharata, Draupadi when she was being stripped naked. As long as she had any pride that she could help herself she was up against Dushyashana, a mighty warrior thousands of times stronger than her. But when she realized I have no defense against this monstrous person, and in a completely pride-less state of mind she threw out her arms as she chanted the name of God “He Krishna, He Govinda”. Thakur was there to help. This is the fact. We must have this faith.

The name of God in every religion is said to be non-different to God himself whether you call him Allah, Jehovah, Yahweh, Christ, Krishna, Hari Rama Govinda. The name of God is not different to God. In the Bible it says chant the name of God and you will be saved. In the Vedas it is said

harer nāma harer nāma
harer nāmaiva kevalam
kalau nāsty eva nāsty eva
nāsty eva gatir anyathā

[Cc Ādi 7.76]

[ ‘For spiritual progress in this Age of Kali, there is no alternative, there is no alternative, there is no alternative to the holy name, the holy name, the holy name of the Lord.’]

There is no other greater means of deliverance from all suffering than chanting the name of god. There are so many people chanting the name of God but how many chanting with feeling, bhakti with dependence, hopelessness and how many are calling out the name of God with devotion?
